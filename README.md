# What is *Souark*?
*Souark* is a fictional sport that I invented for the book I'm writing myself.
It's a mix between boxing, handball and football (soccer).

# How to play a game of *Souark*
There are 2 teams of 2 players, on a field divided in 2 parts.
The game starts at the center of the field, with 1 ball between the teams.
At first, they are in the **conflict zone**, where they have to shoot the ball with their feet.
In that zone, it is allowed to punch the opponents, as long as it is not in the face.
When they arrive in the **throw zone**, they have to take the ball by hand, and throw it in the **cage**, a 1m² rectangle at 1 meter from the ground.
The opponents are obviously allowed to guard the cage.
If you want to pass the ball to your partner, you can only do it when they are in the air first.
The first team to mark 3 points win a set, and the first one to win 2 sets win the match.

This work gave me the idea of doing the Souark API.
